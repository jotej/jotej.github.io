“We discover the structure of the world by observing it, not by being told the name of every object.” — *Yann LeCun, Yoshua Bengio & Geoffrey Hinton*, **Nature** (2015)

Labels are a lossy projection of reality. They are the world squeezed through a tiny semantic lens. That lens can simplify what is seen, but it also discards nuance and structure that labels cannot capture.
